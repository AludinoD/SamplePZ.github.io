Current Game Features : B41

Flee The Horde

Thousands of ‘proper’ zeds roam the streets. Gunfire and combat could lead to a bite...

Sandbox Survival

Scavenge food, craft items, and construct buildings and defences in your fight against inevitable death.

Join Your Friends

Team up with similarly doomed buddies on user-created servers and in split-screen! Trust nobody

Epic Exploration

Explore the towns of isometric Kentucky in our vast, growing map. Where will you make your stand?

Learn To Survive

Develop skills through play, while fending off depression, boredom and infections.

Modding Support

Powerful Lua mod support, with map and dev tools released to the community.

Planned Features

Here are some of the planned features – alongside more weapons, animations, recipes, locations, survival systems and the introduction of dogs. We like dogs. Oh, and maybe the military will turn up one day too. Who can say?

PZ Stories

Our Kate and Baldspot stories will return alongside new plotlines. (Playable in build 0.1.5d)

NPC Survivors

In-depth NPC encounters driven by a metaverse system that tracks off-screen survival.

How It's Released

Project Zomboid will be continually updated until we achieve the feature list we set out at the start of development.

The stable build can be found on Steam and GOG, but we often release Public Test Builds of upcoming updates and patches that are downloadable by opting into the ‘Unstable’ beta on Steam. (When possible these are also updated as betas to GOG).

Please be warned however that the unstable beta may well be buggy – these releases are our way of widening our tester group. For more information keep tabs on the PZ Updates section of our forum.
